ASTRONOMY
FOR
TODDLERS

To Astrophel
on his first birthday.
Look with me
and you can see
the stars up in the sky
And if you squint
you get a hint
of pictures way up high
This one's a goat,
this one's a boat,
and this one here's a hare.
A dophin, some twins,
an Indian,
a queen of Egypt's hair.
A clock, a cup,
a bear, a cub,
a unicorn and pegasus.
A hero, a monster,
a king, a queen,
a princess in distress.
And then there's dawn,
and then you yawn,
the stars have gone to bed.
But now you know,
the stars can show,
a story in your head.
